What's the deal with The Cloverfield Paradox?
The Cloverfield Paradox is an exciting sci-fi adventure/horror film largely beset by the fact that none of it makes any goddamned sense at all. How does the void of space exert a gravitational pull? How does the space station have access to data storage from a different space station it had no contact with and crashed into an ocean years before? The point of making space station segments that spin around is to simulate a gravitational pull, but it pushes you up against the wall, like the flying saucer carnival rides. Moreover, how does anyone actually get inside those spinning segments?

Once you accept that this sci-fi movie has no science and exists only as a delivery vehicle for creepout moments, it's okay, I guess.
